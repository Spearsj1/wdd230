Welcome to Carrollton Texas

Carrollton Texas was founded in the 1840's though the name was not officially established until May 16, 1878. In the early days of the towns existance the two main exports were cotton and grain. Once the 20th centurey rolled around the major industry was bricks. The first telephone came in 1890 and electricity came in 1913. The first railroad came in 1879 and the first electric train in 1923. The first school was opened in 1856 in the Union Baptist Church, where the Perry Cemetary. The first official school building was built in 1890. The Historic Downtown Carrollton Square began to come to shape in 1900 and was a very large center of business and life by 1913. Carrollton became a general law city on June 14, 1913. Some major events that are coming up are the Christmas Party at the A. W. Perry Homestead on 12/16/2021 at 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM at the A. W. Perry Homestead Museum. Another event coming up is the Mayor's Holly Jolly Bike Ride that is happening at Jimmy Porter Park on 12/18/2021 from 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM
