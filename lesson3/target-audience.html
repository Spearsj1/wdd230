Target Audeince: My target audience is going to be adults in between the ages of 18 and 60 who are wanting to get acurate weather information quickly.

Persona 1:

Bio: A middle aged father that wants the best for his family. He is safety consciece and wants to make sure that they will be prepared for whatever weather they come across.

Persona 2:

Bio: A single woman in her early twenties who wants to make sure that the weather will not cause a problem for her while hanging out with friends. She is very social and wants to make sure that her time will not be wasted because of the weather.

Scenarios:

  • What will the weather be like this weekend?
  • Is the weather O.K. in Salt Lake City today?
  • Will it be sunny when we go to the beach on Saturday?
  • How bad is it going to snow today?
  • Will the weather be bad enough to delay my flight?
